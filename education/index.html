About This Assessment

The Education Customer Experience Index will capture feedback from:

  • Primary & Secondary Schools — Teaching quality, facilities, support
  • Vocational Institutions — Skills training, job readiness, support
  • Universities — Academic quality, campus life, career support

This enables educational institutions to understand stakeholder needs and improve learning outcomes across Ghana.
