Customer Experience Intelligence Across Ghana

Ghana CX provides a structured platform for people to evaluate their experience with institutions across major service sectors.

Customer feedback is captured across the moments that shape real experience — access, digital interaction, service quality, responsiveness, trust, transparency, problem resolution and overall satisfaction.

The objective is to transform customer experience from isolated opinion into structured evidence that can support benchmarking, service improvement and institutional accountability.

What Ghana CX Measures

Customer experience across 10 core dimensions

Access & Convenience

Ease of reaching services and institutions

Digital Experience

Quality of online and mobile channels

Physical Service Experience

In-person branch and office experience

Customer Service

Quality of staff and support interactions

Speed & Efficiency

Response times and service delivery

Product / Service Value

Relevance and quality of offerings

Trust & Security

Data safety and institutional reliability

Transparency

Clear communication and disclosure

Problem Resolution

How issues are handled and resolved

Overall Experience

Aggregate satisfaction and likelihood to recommend

Why Customer Experience Matters

For Customers

  • Voice — Your experience is heard and recorded
  • Evidence — Structured feedback supports improvement
  • Better Services — Insights drive service evolution
  • Accountability — Institutions respond to evidence

For Institutions

  • Insight — Understand real customer experience
  • Benchmarking — Compare performance with peers
  • Priority Setting — Focus on high-impact improvements
  • Service Improvement — Data-driven service evolution

Privacy and Independence

Ghana CX is designed to collect and analyse customer experience feedback responsibly. Respondents are informed about the information requested and the purpose of collection through the applicable survey and privacy notice.

Independent Initiative: Ghana CX is an independent customer experience measurement initiative, not a regulatory authority or official national benchmark. We operate transparently and are committed to data protection and respondent privacy.
